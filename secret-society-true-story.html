Is Secret Society Movie A True Story?

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Is Secret Society Movie A True Story? No, Secret Society movie is not a true story.

Jamal Hill directed the fictional drama picture with his co-screenplay with Miasha Coleman. It is based 2006 best-selling novel of the same name by Coleman.

After two years, the author produced the stage play adaptation of her novel with the help of her husband, Rich Coleman. Basically, the tale is full of high fashion, high lifestyle, and even higher drama.

The fashion model Reyna Love and Erica Pinkett are the main leads. Makers want the audience to see the struggles that epicene people face in their daily life.

Within around a year, the picture's second chapter was introduced. Almost all the actors from the 2021 cinema are featured in the second chapter. 

When Does Secret Society Come Out?

Secret Society release date was March 31 2021 in the United Kingdom. A few days later, the picture broadcasted on April 2 in the United States.

Currently, the cinema can be streamed on Tubi and Amazon Prime Video. Unfortunately, it is not available on Netflix.

Trailer ( Source : youtube )

The drama, with a 1 hour and 34 minutes running time, has earned an IMDb ranking of 4.6 out of 10 based on 615 users. Regarding its review, the audience found the cinema quite entertaining.

One of the viewers wrote, " This movie really opens your eyes to seeing things from a different perspective. Great acting, directing, wardrobe. Thumbs up, I would recommend."

Cast And Crew

Cast of Secret Society film includes Reyna Love, Erica Pinkett Jeremy Meeks and others. Vincent De Paul and Tyler Hopkins are also in the picture.

Two females are in the main lead of the cinema. They have nailed their roles with realistic acting and dialogue delivery. The protagonists of the picture team up together to trick the male characters into luring their money.

With the money they get, they spend them on shopping for luxurious goods. But once their jaw-dropping secret from the past comes to the surface, they are about to face hurdles in life.

Let's find out more about the actors and their role-play in detail.

Reyna Love as Celess

The fashion diva, Reyna, portrays the character of Celess in the picture. Her original name is Charlie.

Celess is a good friend of Tina. Both of them are passionate about fashion and high-standard lifestyles. 

The social media influencer started her professional career as a fashion model. She has been featured in music videos and campaigns for companies like Erico Palace and Chic Couture.

Moreover, she has participated in beauty pageants in her initial career phase. Later, she rose to prominence on social media handles. The actress has over 123 thousand followers on her Instagram account.

On the personal front, Love is the younger sister of hip-hop artist Moneybo. She was born on April 25, 1992, in Miami, Florida.

Erica Pinkett as Tina

Triple D Revenge star appeared as Tina in the drama picture. Her character's original name is Thomas, and she becomes a close friend of Celess.

Tina and her best friend get a minor cosmetic surgery to enhance their beauty as they always imagined. With her new look, she starts doing everything, like buying luxury goods and living on the top of the world with the money they earned while tricking the male.

The television personality is prominent for her roles in When It Rains, Recognize 2, and Unfair Exchange. Besides, she has appeared in cinemas like Single Not Searching, Chedda Boys, and All Eyez On Me.

The Boston-born actress is active on social media sites. Over 43 thousand people follow her on Twitter, while that on Instagram is 437 thousand.

Jeremy Meeks as Khalil

In the cinema, we can see Jeremy Meeks playing Khalil.

Jeremy began his acting career in 2020 with Trigger, in which he played the role of Trey Mass. The same year, his next project, True to the Game 2, hit the cinema theatres. Some of his notable performances can be seen in Rise of the Tarragon, Unfair Exchange, and Dutch.

The Doggmen actor was born in Stockton, California on February 7, 1984. His friends call him with his nickname "The Blue-Eyed Bandit."

On the personal front, he is a father of three children. His wife Melissa filed for divorce after around 14 years of marriage since December 27, 2008.

Vincent De Paul as Derek Rossi

Vincent De Paul played Derek Rossi in the fictional picture. He has an appearance in both chapters of the cinema.

Born on September 2, 1968, in Baltimore, Maryland, the actor is the son of a mortuary scientist father and a socialite mother.

Maid in Manhattan Star began his acting career in 1988 with Hairspray. To date, he has worked on hundreds of films and television series. Some of his renowned projects are Poseidon, This Is Our Christmas, Contadora Is For Lovers, and Tesla.

There are around 12 cinemas and television series which are on the list of his upcoming. Last year, his cinemas like Hairball, The Gunslinger Grifter Logan, and Nothing Is Impossible released. Moreover, he played the role of father for an episode in the television series When Sharks Attack.

In decades of his contribution to the showbiz industry, the actor has earned numerous accolades. In 2012, he won Beverly Hills Film Festival Award as Best Actor for his performance in Silver Case. Similarly, he is the Spooky Empire Award nominee 2022 for Best Scare Actor.

Never Enough

Secret Society Movie 2021 Part 2 was released on July 29 2022 in the United States. Jamal Hill and Miasha Coleman came together once again for the 2nd chapter.

In the second section, Adejah Parrish joined the team as Si Si. Celess is back with newfound hunger. She has made new buddies. They continue their old business but make it even bigger.

The fictionalized film had a red carpet-premiere in July 2022. The film cast and many others attended the event.

It takes a few moments for them to realize the bigger they get, the harder they fall. And all thanks to Celess's past.
