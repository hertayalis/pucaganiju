Is Breece Hall Playing in Week 1 vs. Bills: Fantasy Outlook of Jets RB (MNF 2023)

Publish date: 2024-06-26

The Jets face the Buffalo Bills on Monday Night Football in Week 1. The Bills, known for their stout defense, will pose a challenge for Hall, especially coming off a significant injury. In this article, we will have a look at key factors affecting Breece Hall’s Week 1 availability.

Breece Hall’s Injury Status for Week 1


Breece Hall, a prominent second-round pick by the Jets, had a stellar start to his NFL journey before an ACL tear against the Denver Broncos in Week 7 of the previous season halted his momentum. While he began training camp on the Physically Unable to Perform (PUP) list, he made a notable return to practice by Aug. 15. His progress is promising, pointing towards a potential return in the Jets’ 2023 opener.

Jets RB Breece Hall: Fantasy Impact Analysis vs. Bills


The shadow of Hall’s injury might affect his explosive playing style, at least initially. Fantasy football managers need to weigh the risks and rewards. The addition of Dalvin Cook to the roster complicates matters, suggesting that the Jets might distribute the workload between the two backs.

Given the uncertainties, a conservative projection might place Hall at 50-60 yards rushing, with potential added value in PPR formats if he’s involved in the passing game.

Is Breece Hall a Viable Fantasy Option for MNF 2023?


Considering the competition in the backfield and his recent return from injury, it might be risky to depend on Hall as an RB1. However, in deeper leagues or as a flex play, he might provide value. If your league is standard-sized, and you have better, proven options, it might be worth benching Hall for Week 1. However, if you’re in a deeper league or require a flex play, Hall might be worth the gamble.

Alternative Fantasy Options at RB for MNF 2023

While Hall’s recovery has been positive, the Jets may prioritize his long-term health over immediate gains. The presence of Dalvin Cook also allows the team to manage Hall’s snaps strategically.


Dalvin Cook, also on the Jets’ roster, is an obvious alternative, especially since his role is yet undefined. Outside the Jets, exploring other matches for the week may yield other suitable RB candidates for MNF.

Final Thoughts


The excitement surrounding Breece Hall’s return is palpable. Yet, fantasy football managers should exercise caution in Week 1, considering both the competition in the Jets’ backfield and Hall’s return from a significant injury. The matchup against the Bills isn’t easy, but Hall’s potential cannot be ignored. Weighing the pros and cons will be crucial for fantasy success.
