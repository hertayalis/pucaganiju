Is Valtteri Bottas a soldier?

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Is Valtteri Bottas a soldier? – Valtteri Viktor Bottas is a Finnish racing driver who currently competes in Formula One for Alfa Romeo.

He previously drove for Mercedes from 2017 to 2021 and Williams from 2013 to 2016. Bottas has 10 race wins and 67 podiums to his name and contributed to five constructors championship wins for Mercedes. He was also a two-time drivers’ championship runner-up in 2019 and 2020.

Bottas started his racing career in karting and later progressed to single-seater championships, winning the Formula Renault Eurocup and the Formula Renault Northern European Cup in 2008. In 2009, he moved up to the Formula 3 Euroseries, where he finished third in the championship and won the Masters of Formula 3 event.

He joined Williams in 2013 as a full-time driver, finishing on the podium nine times during his time with the team and achieving a top championship position of fourth in 2014. After Nico Rosberg’s retirement, Bottas joined Mercedes as his replacement to partner Lewis Hamilton.

Apart from racing, Bottas co-owns a coffee roastery called “Kahiwa Coffee Roasters” in Lahti, Finland. He also launched the Valtteri Bottas Duathlon in 2017, an annual sporting event that raises money for charity and various good causes.

In 2022, he partnered with his girlfriend to launch Oath, a premier gin inspired by their family heritages of Finland and Australia. Bottas is also a co-founder and partner of FNLD GRVL, a gravel cycling event in Lahti, Finland, where he is partnered with Amy Charity of SBT GRVL and Tiffany Cromwell.

In 2019, Valtteri Bottas participated in the Arctic Rally, winning a stage and finishing fifth overall. He also won the Rallycircuit Côte d’Azur held in Paul Ricard in December of the same year. The following year, he took part in the Arctic Lapland Rally, driving a Citroën DS3 WRC car and finishing ninth. In October 2020, he underwent an Extreme E test with Jean-Éric Vergne and Sébastien Loeb. In 2021, he competed in his third Arctic Lapland Rally and secured a class podium, finishing sixth overall.

Although Bottas was originally set to participate in the Race of Champions in 2022 with two-time Formula One champion Mika Häkkinen, he withdrew from the event at the last minute. Instead, he is scheduled to race alongside Häkkinen in the 2023 edition of the competition.

Is Valtteri Bottas a soldier?

Valtteri Bottas received his education in Heinola, Finland. As is mandatory for adult males in Finland, he served in the army for a short period and held the rank of lance corporal.
