How many children does Lionel Richie have?

Lionel Richie has three children from two marriages.

In 1975, Richie married his college sweetheart, Brenda Harvey.

Eight years later, the couple adopted their daughter Nicole Camille Escovedo, now Nicole Richie.

When Nicole was three-years-old, her biological parents agreed to let her move in with Richie, because they could not afford to provide for her.
