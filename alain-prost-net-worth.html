Alain Prost Net Worth: $100 Million

Alain Prost is a French race car driver who is a four-time Formula One Driver’s Champion and has a net worth of $100 million. Prost’s 51 Grand Prix victories held the record from 1987 to 2001. He was awarded the World Sports Awards of the Century for the motor sport category in 1999.

Prost discovered karting at the age of 14 and won the French and European Formula Three championships. He joined the McLaren Formula One racing team in 1980 at the age of 24 and won his first race for the Renault team a year later at the Grand Prix in France.

Prost was known for his fierce rivalry with driver Ayrton Senna, which included controversial clashes. He was fired from Ferrari after criticizing his team in public. Prost retired from Formula One after winning the 1993 championship with the Williams team. He then took over the French Liger team in 1997, running it as Prost Grand Prix until 2002 when it went bankrupt.

Prost now competes in the Andros Trophy, an ice racing championship, and completed the Absa Cape Epic race in 2012 and 2013. He is married to Anne-Marie and the couple has two sons together. Prost was awarded the Legion d’Honneur by President Francois Mitterrand.
