Brooklyn Beckham Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Who is Brooklyn Beckham?

Brooklyn Beckham is a British model and photographer. He is one of the children of famous soccer player David Beckham and fashionista Victoria Beckham.

Brooklyn Beckham: Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Siblings

He was born on 4 March 1999, in West End, England, as Brooklyn Joseph Beckham. His ethnicity is mixed (English, Jewish, and German).

He is the son of David Beckham (father) and Victoria Beckham (mother). His father is a former most loved footballer and his mother is a former spice girl.

He is the eldest one with three younger siblings, Romeo, Cruz, and Harper.

In which school did Brooklyn Beckham attend?

Talking about his education, he went to Parsons School of Design in Greenwich Village.

Brooklyn Beckham: Professional Life, Career

Brooklyn Beckham, being from a family of modeling and football talent, running in his veins, from an early age, has featured in TV documentaries The Real Beckham and Victoria Beckham.

He made his modeling debut in “Man About Town” magazine in March 2014. After this, he was featured in overs of magazines like Vogue China, Miss Vogue, Interview, L’Uomo Vogue, T: The New York Times Style Magazine, and Dazed Korea.

He was first seen on television at the age of three. As a photographer, he shot for the campaign of Burberry BRIT.

It was disclosed that Beckham was pursuing his career as a professional chief in 2021.

Salary and Net Worth

As of 2023, this TV star’s estimated net worth is $10 million. Brooklyn Beckham was chosen by a brand like Huawei Honor 8 Android phones to appear in their advertisements.

His father David and mother Victoria have a combined net worth of $450 million. They bought three houses, one in 1999 at $3.3M, the second one in 2007 at $18.7M (sold at $33M), and the third one in 2013 at $41M.

Also, this couple has $4 million for a house in the South of France.

Rumors and Controversy

When Brooklyn Beckham was hired at age of 16 by Burberry to shoot the latest fashion then there were controversies that he got it through nepotism.

He is rumored to have hooked up with Madison Beer in 2017 and Tallia Storm in 2014.

How tall is Brooklyn Beckham?
