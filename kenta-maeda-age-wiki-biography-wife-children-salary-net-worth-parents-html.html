Kenta Maeda, also known as Markin, is a Japanese professional baseball player. Currently playing for the Los Angeles Dodgers of Major League Baseball (MLB), he is the youngest player in Japanese baseball history to win the Pitcher Triple Crown in the same year and to win the Sawamura Award twice.

He was born on April 11, 1988 in Takaoka, Osaka, Japan. Maeda graduated from PL Gakuen High School. He started his career with the Hiroshima Toyo Carp’s Nippon Professional Baseball (NPB) and joined the Los Angeles Dodgers in 2016.
