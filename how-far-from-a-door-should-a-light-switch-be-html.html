Just to clarify, how far away from the floor should a light switch be?

Each room in a home should have at least one switch to operate the lights in that room, according to the National Electrical Code. However, the code does not specify how high the switch should be placed. Switch boxes are often installed with the bottom of the box 48 inches above the floor, according to several builders.

Second, what is the best location for a light switch in a sleeping area?

Convention dictates that most switch boxes be built with the bottom of the box positioned between 44 and 46 inches above the floor, resulting in a top of the box that is between 47 1/2 and 49 1/2 inches above the floor on average. By following these norms, the top of the box is less than the 54-inch limit allowed by the ADA for height.
