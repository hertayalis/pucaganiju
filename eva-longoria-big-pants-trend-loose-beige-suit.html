Eva Longoria Embraces the Big-Pants Trend in a Loose Beige Suit

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Eva Longoria stepped out today and did the impossible: She made office style look both chic and comfortable.

The actress and director was photographed arriving for an interview at The View’s headquarters in New York City in a lounge-y beige suit. The set consisted of an oversize tailored blazer and big, loose, high-waisted pants. She layered it simply over a white tank and paired it with shimmering champagne-colored platform pumps by Santoni. Longoria then added some sparkle to the monochrome ensemble with large gold hoop earrings, a gold chain necklace encrusted with tiny diamonds, a gold-and-diamond choker, and a gold bangle. She wore her hair in silky waves and opted for rosy makeup.

Gotham//Getty Images

The Desperate Housewives star has been showing off her sophisticated street- and red-carpet style as of late while celebrating her recent achievements.

Last week, she wore another nine-to-five look, consisting of a monochrome Barbie-pink suit, for a jam-packed day of meetings and events in Chicago. It was composed of a silky long-sleeved top with a mock turtleneck and matching tailored trousers with a ribbon belt. She continued the color scheme with silky pointed-toe pumps in the same shade.

And earlier this month, she attended the premiere of her movie Flamin’ Hot on the opening night of the 2023 Los Angeles Latino International Film Festival. For the occasion, she wore a dramatic black gown. The semi-sheer asymmetrical shirt dress featured long sleeves and a high leg slit on one side. Its long train fell down to the red carpet. Longoria paired it simply with black platform sandals by Christian Louboutin. She additionally accessorized with large diamond drop earrings and silver rings.
