Kristen Stewart and St. Vincent Make Their First Red Carpet Appearance Together

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Getty Images

Kristen Stewart and St. Vincent made their first official public appearance as a couple when they attended a CFDA event together last night. The duo exuded an edgy kind of cool with their matching shades and tailored ensembles. Stewart, a Chanel muse, paired a blazer with houndstooth trousers and patent leather loafers while St. Vincent, better knowns as Annie Clark, went for a cropped tweed jacket, wide-leg pants and a choker.

Getty Images

They even rubbed shoulders with Emma Stone and Katy Perry, who both looked radiant in white.

Getty Images

News of the actress and indie singer's relationship first broke two weeks ago, after they were seen spending time together in New York—also in matching outfits. Be on the lookout for more coordinated fashion moments from this Cool Girl couple.

Their new romance caused a stir since it shortly followed both parties' most recent relationships. Stewart was last seen with former girlfriend Alicia Cargile in late August, and Clark split from Cara Delevingne in September after a year and a half together.
