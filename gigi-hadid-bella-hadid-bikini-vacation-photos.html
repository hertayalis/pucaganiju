Gigi Hadid Wears a Thong Bikini While Bella Hadid Dons One-Piece

Publish date: 2024-08-07
  • Supermodel sisters Gigi and Bella Hadid are currently on vacation in Mykonos, Greece, to celebrate their sister Alana Hadid's 34th birthday.
  • Gigi and Bella both took to Instagram to share some seriously revealing swimwear shots, with both Victoria's Secret models looking as flawless as ever.
  • While Gigi wore a thong bikini by Danielle Guizio, Bella's eye-catching one-piece was designed by Louisa Ballou.

The Hadid sisters regularly share swimwear snaps on Instagram, so the new photos to come out of their latest vacation should come as no surprise.
