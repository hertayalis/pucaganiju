Helen Flanagan looks unreal as she models striking corset top

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Former Coronation Street star Helen Flanagan wowed in the Ann Summers campaign

    Helen Flanagan got herself into the Easter mood for a new campaign with Ann Summers and the star looked sensational in a selection of daring looks.

    As you can see in the video below, the former actress looked beautiful in every single outfit from white lacy corsets to pink lingerie sets to one that even saw her don a pair of rabbit ears, bow-tie and fishnet stockings.

    In her caption, she teasingly wrote: "Ready for the Easter bunny," alongside a rabbit, bar of chocolate and love heart emoji.

    Her fans lavished her with compliments in the comments, as one enthused: "Beauty inside and out," and a second shared: "YOU ARE SO GORGEOUS HELS! Christ on a bike."

    Helen is no stranger to a racy shoot, and last week she stunned fans with another striking look.The mother-of-three modelled for the camera in the bold leopard print top which she teamed with a pair of blue wash jeans. Accessorising to perfection, Helen completed the look with a gold chain necklace with a pair of gold drop earrings.
