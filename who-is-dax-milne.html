Who is Dax Milne? | The US Sun

Publish date: 2024-05-21

COMMANDOS star Dax Milne has been caught in the crossfire amid explosive allegations about his former college roommate Zach Wilson.

Bombshell claims were made by his girlfriend and Wilson's ex - Abbey Gile - as they confirmed their relationship online.

Who is Dax Milne?

Dax Milne is an American wide receiver for NFL side the Washington Commanders.

He was drafted in the 2021 draft following a successful spell of college football at Bingham Young University (BYU).

During his glittering school career, the receiver, who hails from South Jordan, Utah, was a member of three state championship teams in football and two in basketball.

Milne played in 10 games with three starts in his freshman season and finished the year with 69 yards and one touchdown on 10 receptions.

He caught 21 passes for 285 yards and two touchdowns as a sophomore.

Milne later decided to miss a senior season to enter the NFL draft – signing a four-year rookie contract with the Commandos in May 2021.

Is Dax Milne dating Abbey Gile?

Following intense social media speculation, Milne confirmed his relationship with Utah Valley University college student Abbey Gile in July 2022.

Uploading a romantic photo of the couple holding hands on the beach on Instagram, he captioned the post: "Word on the street."

The pair have reportedly known each other since college when Gile used to date Milne’s former roommate - New York Jets quarterback Zach Wilson.

According to the New York Post, Gile and Wilson had been in a relationship since high school.

But the couple split in January 2022 as explosive allegations later emerged via Gile that the Jets quarterback had slept with his mom's best friend.

The shocking claim was made after one user labelled Gile as a "homie hopper" in the comments of the loved-up snap.

Abbey then fired back, claiming that Wilson was the reason behind their split as he had the affair behind her back.

She said: "He was sleeping with his mom's best friend... that's the real homie hopper."

Gile has since reportedly deleted the comment.

Are Dax Milne and Zach Wilson friends?

Zach and Dax’s friendship reportedly goes back to their childhood and they have known each other since they were eight years old.

As children, Zach and Dax played on different football teams and were also on opposite basketball teams.

Speaking on their friendship, Dax previously told The Salt Lake Tribune: “I just respected him because of how much of a competitor he was.

“And then, you know how it is, once you start being on the same team, you start kind of growing a brotherhood. And then a friendship grew.”

The bromance continued beyond childhood and they both studied at BYU before joining separate NFL teams.
