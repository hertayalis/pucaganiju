RHOC season 16 episode 11 isnt on tonight, but the air date isnt far off Thu 24 February

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Shannon Beador, Heather Dubrow, Emily Simpson and the rest of the Orange County ladies are back for a 16th instalment of Bravo’s Real Housewives of Orange County in 2022. The show is in full swing and has been serving all kinds of drama since episode 1 kicked off on December 1st, 2021.

From Noella Bergener’s divorce to tensions rising as Heather settles back into the group, there really hasn’t been a dull moment since RHOC has aired in 2022. Here’s more on whether The Real Housewives of Orange County season 16 episode 11 is on tonight (February 23rd).

The Real Housewives of Orange County season 16 episode 11

RHOC fans may be unimpressed on Wednesday, February 23rd 2022 as The Real Housewives of Orange County season 16 episode 11 won’t be airing.

The show is usually on each Wednesday and has been airing since December 1st, 2021.

The last episode that aired ‘Edible Derangements’ aired on February 16th, but between episodes 10 and 11 there’s a two-week wait.

RHOC season 16 episode 11 explored

Airing on March 2nd, 2022, RHOC season 16 episode 11 sees Noella finally get some clarity on what’s going on with her divorce. Episode 10 saw Emily say that Noella’s divorce was “all she talked about“, so with any luck for Emily, it’ll be a thing of the past soon.

After the drama of episode 10, Dr Jen’s relationship with Ryne is looking to be on the mend and the idea of divorce left by the wayside. Heather and Gina become even better friends and the rumours of Shannon being jealous are aired.

Emily prepares for her daughter’s baptism in episode 11 which gets her very emotional and Gina and her ex appear to be on amicable terms.

Why isn’t RHOC on this week?

RHOC season 16 episode 11 isn’t airing in its usual Wednesday slot as The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills reunion is taking place from 7 pm.

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills season 11 episode 21 is airing back to back on Bravo on Wednesday, February 23rd.

All four parts of the reunion are airing until 11 pm.
